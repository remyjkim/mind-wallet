# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want participation in the `mindwallet` project to be respectful, technically rigorous, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Assume good intent, but hold technical claims to a high standard.
- Help keep public documentation and examples accurate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Deliberate misinformation
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove or reject contributions that violate them.
